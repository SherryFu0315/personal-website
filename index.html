<header>
<h1 id="aboutme">About me</h1>
<p> I am now working as associate professor and Department co-Chair at the <a
href="http://www.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/">Department of Computer Science and
Technology</a> in Tsinghua University,
Beijing, China. My major research interests are in Web Search, User Behavior Analysis, and Natural
Language Processing.
I am also a visiting research associate professor of National University of Singapore and a visiting
associate professor of National Institute of Informatics (NII) of Japan,
as well as a member of <a href="http://tiangong.sogou.com">Tiangong AI Research Center</a> which is
founded by Tsinghua and Sogou Inc.</p>


</header>
